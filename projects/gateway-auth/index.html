Gateway and authorization experiments

A set of backend projects around gateway boundaries, service access, and Casbin-based authorization.

GoCasbinAPI GatewayObservability Source

Problem

Backend projects become easier to reason about when gateway and authorization boundaries are explicit. This work collects experiments around policy checks, service entry points, and operational visibility.

Direction

The goal is to keep authorization decisions explainable while making the integration points boring:

  • policy definitions are reviewable
  • service boundaries are visible
  • failures are observable
  • local development stays lightweight
